v0.7 — "Reputation Matters"

Reputation now actually affects who signs up.

Released Day 0 of Season 3 Build 18,402 👥 2,841 players online 💬 412 comments

You asked: "why does my 12-rep garage attract the same enterprise customers as paulwall?" Fair. We rewrote the customer acquisition model so reputation, region, and price tier all bias the pool of leads you see in Marketplace. Karen is no longer guaranteed to find you.

Also: every NPC customer now remembers your past tickets across save files. Yes, Karen will hold a grudge. We're sorry. Continue reading the highlights ↓

NEW
Public reputation page. Every player has a /rep URL showing uptime, response time, last 10 reviews. Linkable in Marketplace.
BAL
Customer churn tuned. Loyal personas (Devon-type) tolerate 2 P2s/month; Karen-type tolerates none. Adjust your shift coverage.
FIX
Ticket inbox no longer freezes when 200+ tickets stack from a regional outage event. (Sorry, paulwall.)
BRK
Pre-Season 3 saves migrate on load. Old reputation numbers will shift up to ±20 to fit the new scale.
